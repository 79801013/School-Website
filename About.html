About Eastview Highschool


Established in 1863 by the visionary mathematician and engineer Alexander Eastview, Eastview High School and Sixth Form has stood as a symbol of academic excellence and innovation for over 160 years. With its proud heritage and forward-thinking approach, our school bridges tradition with modernity, creating an environment where students flourish academically, socially, and personally.

Eastview is more than just a school—it is a thriving community. We offer cutting-edge facilities, from state-of-the-art science laboratories and a fully equipped library to modern sports complexes and creative arts studios. These resources enable our students to explore their passions, hone their skills, and achieve their fullest potential.

Our alumni serve as living proof of the enduring legacy of Eastview High School and Sixth Form. Among our graduates are renowned scientists, celebrated artists, compassionate leaders, and trailblazers in countless industries. Their achievements inspire our current students to aim high and make a meaningful impact.

At Eastview, diversity and inclusion are at the heart of everything we do. We take pride in fostering an environment where all students feel respected, valued, and empowered. Through our dynamic curriculum and rich array of extracurricular opportunities—including award-winning debate teams, performing arts programs, and competitive sports—we nurture well-rounded individuals who are prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.

We are deeply committed to preparing our students for the future. From personalized career guidance and university preparation programs to entrepreneurship initiatives, Eastview equips learners with the tools they need to thrive in an ever-evolving world. Above all, we strive to instill in them the resilience, integrity, and work ethic necessary to become conscientious, hardworking adults who contribute meaningfully to society.

With a history steeped in excellence and a vision focused on progress, Eastview High School and Sixth Form continues to empower generations of learners to lead with confidence, curiosity, and compassion.
