Eastview Highschool and Sixth Form


Eastview Highschool and Sixth Form was founded in the era of intelligence; The Victorian Era. It was founded in 1863 by Alexander Eastview when he was 39, on the basis that it would brighten the lives of uneducated children and bring out that innermost passion for learning and striving for greatness. With us you can help your child prosper and flourish with the help of our centuries long method of learning and with teachers of the highest order, we will give your child the tools of greatness. Join us in our mission to fill our world with bright minds for generations to come. Join Eastview.

Latest Clubs

Ruby Rockets

Here at Ruby Rockets United, we are offering kids aged 11 till 17 to join and we will support their potential football careers the best we can,referring them to higher division football teams and even academies! We will regularly set friendly matches against other schools and even host tournaments. We will have training on both Tuesday and Thursday after school from 3:10pm to 4:10pm.

Phoenix Stride

Here at Pheonix we offer a multitude of gymnastics skills and knowledge to kids ages 11-17 that will benefit your child in the future. We offer flexibility practice, dance, games, and a bunch of other gymnastics related content that will boost your child up in their possible career of gymnastics. We host our club on Mondays and Wednesdays after school from 3:10 to 4:10.

Music Masters

Here at Music Masters, we offer singing practice children ages 11-14 to boost your child's singing skills and ways to enhance their range capacity that will benefit your child in the future. We offer a newfound method of curiosity that could eventually lead on to your child singing in our school plays/pantomimes, and even at our concerts! We host our club after school on Fridays from 3:10 to 4:10.
