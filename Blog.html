Eastview Students Win National Mathematics Championship

Eastview High School has triumphed at this year's Annual Mathematics Competition, emerging as champions after months of dedication and perseverance. This achievement is a testament to the students' unwavering commitment to excellence, their collaborative spirit, and the guidance of their inspiring mentors. From late-night problem-solving sessions to navigating challenging concepts, their journey exemplifies hard work and passion. This victory not only celebrates their brilliance in mathematics but also showcases the power of teamwork and determination in overcoming obstacles. Congratulations to the Eastview team for this well-deserved milestone!
